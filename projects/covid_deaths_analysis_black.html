Business Objective

Governments, healthcare organizations, and policymakers faced critical challenges during the pandemic:

  • Tracking Global Impact: Monitor total cases and deaths across regions.
  • Resource Allocation: Identify continents and countries with the highest mortality burden.
  • Forecasting Infections: Understand infection trajectories to anticipate healthcare needs.
  • Public Awareness: Provide clear, data-driven insights to inform public policy and citizen behavior.

The objective of this dashboard is to consolidate COVID-19 data into actionable insights for better decision-making, preparedness, and transparent communication.

Tools & Technologies

  • SQL: Data extraction, transformation, and cleaning.
  • Tableau: Interactive dashboards, KPI visualizations, geo-mapping, forecasting.
  • Geo-Mapping: Map layer with OpenStreetMap/Mapbox.
  • Forecasting Model: Time-series projection for infection percentages.

Insights & Recommendations

  • Europe & Americas: Require continued monitoring and resource allocation due to higher death counts.
  • Developing Regions: Lower reported deaths may reflect limited access or underreporting; invest in testing and reporting infrastructure.
  • U.S. & U.K.: Trends highlight the importance of vaccination campaigns and early public health interventions.
  • Policy Action: Use forecasted growth to strengthen hospital capacity, ensure vaccine supply, and implement timely containment measures.

Real-World Applications

  • Healthcare Systems: Anticipate ICU bed demand and ventilator needs.
  • Government Agencies: Guide policy on restrictions, aid distribution, and vaccination rollouts.
  • Public Communication: Build awareness about infection risks and encourage compliance.
  • Global Coordination: Facilitate collaboration between nations to share vaccines and resources.
