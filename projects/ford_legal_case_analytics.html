Ford Motor Company — Legal Case Management & Budget Analytics

Tech Stack

SQL · Python (Pandas, NumPy) · Tableau · ETL Automation · Scheduling

Project Snapshot

  • Developed a case tracking & budget monitoring system across 50+ outside counsel firms to improve visibility into progress and financial performance.
  • Automated ingestion from case logs with SQL/Python; reduced manual status updates by 70%.
  • Tableau dashboards improved on-time case resolution by 25% and accelerated monthly reporting from 2 weeks → 2 days.
  • KPIs (budget variance, spend per case, avg. resolution time) enabled 15% savings in counsel contracts.
  • Real-time alerts for overruns & delays cut response time by 30%.

Business Problem

  • Hundreds of active cases with outside counsel.
  • Low visibility into firm assignment, progress timelines, and budget adherence.
  • Manual spreadsheets led to delays, errors, and weak performance tracking.

Use Case Diagram

Actors: Legal Handler, Outside Counsel, Leadership

Solution Overview

1) Data Ingestion & Processing
Pulled data from Ford’s case management system into SQL tables; cleaned & standardized case records (ID, firm, budget, spend-to-date, deadlines). Automated daily ETL with Python (Pandas, NumPy).

2) Metrics & KPIs
Defined KPIs: Budget variance (actual – allocated), on-time completion rate, avg. resolution time per firm, cost per case across firms.

3) Visualization in Tableau
Built: Case Tracker (stage), Budget Monitor (spend vs allocation), Firm Comparison (benchmarking), Backlog Report (stuck/delayed). Filters for firm, case type, region.

4) Automation & Alerts
Notifications for budget overruns & delayed cases; proactive triage and remediation.

Impact

  • Manual tracking effort reduced by 70%.
  • On-time case resolution improved by 25%.
  • 15% savings negotiated with underperforming firms using benchmarks.
  • Monthly reporting time cut from 2 weeks to 2 days.
  • Executive visibility into 100+ active cases at a glance.
